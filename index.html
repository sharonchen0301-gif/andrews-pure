Our Story

Flavor without shortcuts.

Andrew grew up in China and spent years traveling across the world, carrying the cuisines of his childhood with him — rich Chinese flavors, the kind of cooking where a sauce can make or break a dish.

While perfecting his own personal sauces and seasonings (flavors he eventually wanted to bring to the world) he found that several recipes required a deep peanut flavor. But every powder on the market had been defatted, stripping out precisely the richness that makes peanuts worth cooking with.

"The ones on the market tasted like they'd had their peanut essence taken out. And that's because they had."

So Andrew found his own method — one that preserved the oils, locked in the roasted aroma, could be stored for a long period, and still produced the deep peanut flavor. That became Andrew's Pure. And from the same kitchen, with the same obsession for honest ingredients, came Any Time Bowls — bold crunch oils rooted in the Chinese culinary tradition he grew up with.
