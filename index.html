Our Story

Flavor without shortcuts.

Andrew grew up in China and spent years traveling across the United States, carrying the cuisines of his childhood with him — Chinese, Southeast Asian, the kind of cooking where a sauce can make or break a dish.

While perfecting his own personal sauces and seasonings — flavors he eventually wanted to bring to the world — several recipes required deep peanut flavor. But every powder on the market had been defatted, stripping out precisely the richness that makes peanuts worth cooking with.

"The ones on the market tasted like they'd had the soul taken out. Because they had."

So Andrew found his own method — one that preserved the oils, locked in the roasted aroma, and produced a powder that actually tasted like peanuts. That became Andrew's Pure. And from the same kitchen, with the same obsession for honest ingredients, came Any Time Bowls — bold crunch oils rooted in the Chinese culinary tradition he grew up with.
